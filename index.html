Comparative Analysis & Conclusion

Why the Ensemble Wins

The Stacked Ensemble outperforms individual models by leveraging their complementary strengths. While LSTM excels at capturing long-term temporal dependencies and XGBoost dominates in feature interaction handling, the Ensemble combines them to cancel out individual errors.

LSTM Weakness

Struggles with sudden spikes and high-frequency noise.

XGBoost Strength

Robust to outliers and captures non-linear feature interactions.

The Result: As shown in Figure 4 below, the Ensemble (Green Line) achieves a stable high accuracy (R¬≤ > 0.99), significantly reducing the error variance seen in the standalone LSTM model (Blue Bars).
